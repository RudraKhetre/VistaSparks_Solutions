Our Mission

Novatec IT Training Services provides professional I.T. training courses in order to prepare qualified individuals to launch their careers in this ever changing technology world. Our courses are focused on practical and in house trainings which gives the individual the confidence and exposure of real-world scenarios and problem-solving approach.

Our Company

Novatec IT Training Services is founded by experienced information technology professionals and instructors who has decided to concentrate in the hottest and fastest growing Information Technology (IT) areas: Cloud technology, cutting edge programming languages and networking.

We employ talented instructors, who are seasoned IT professionals and bring depth of real workplace knowledge to their classrooms. The facilities are built with modern training and computer networks keeping in mind every need of the trainers and the students.

Our facility is conveniently located in Wakad near Hinjewadi, which is considered to be the IT hub of Pune. In addition to taking classes, our students are encouraged to use the facility and the expertise as much as they can, even after the course, in order to maintain and advance their skills.

Programs and Faculty

Our approach is to provide rigorous theoretical training while teaching you extensive practical skills. We make every effort to prepare you to face the challenges of today's rapidly evolving information technology marketplace. Our training manuals and exercise materials are very effective in enhancing knowledge and skills base. Our instructors are not just experienced teachers, they are team leaders and senior technical developers who themselves mentor and coach new employees and consultants hired on their projects.

As project leaders, our instructors also interview prospective candidates for permanent and consulting positions. They know first-hand, what skills are needed to start a career in the IT field, pass job interviews, and successfully perform on the first job assignment. Our instructors work very hard and with a lot of personal attention to transfer that knowledge to each student and to keep them well informed in all the latest industry developments.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing the best professional training to you to help launch you onto a growth path in the field of IT. For that reason our company invests in new facilities, the latest software and newest hardware and hires talented trainers and assistants

Our company is not just an institute oblivious to its students somewhere between the last course payment and the graduation. Our ultimate goal is to form long-term relationships with professionals and to help them advance in their IT careers.
